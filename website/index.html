A smart shoe sole to help you keep track of your foot ulcer. We are here for you every step of the way.

Prevent infections and ischemia without having to remove bandages every few minutes.

How we do it

Our SmartSole contains a small device connected to pressure, temperature and movement sensors. 

This allows you to keep track of 3 major wellbeing indicators for your ulcer:

 - Temperature: a typical symptom of an infected ulcer is it getting very hot.

 - Pressure: putting too much pressure on the foot where your ulcer is can be very detrimental to your ulcer. For this reason SmartSole also has a small air pouch to even out the pressure on your foot.

 - Step count: walking too much when you have an ulcer can put too much stress on your foot and result in abnormal blood flow. For this reason keeping track of how much you walk is essential.

Simple Monitoring

Get notified if you are putting too much stress on your foot. Monitor if it is getting too hot and getting enough ventilation for optimal healing.

Encrypted Data Transfer

Protecting your data is our first priority which is why all communications between your device and our servers are encrypted.

Non-Invasive

Simply take your shoes, substitute the sole and connect to the web interface.

No setup needed

As soon as you connect to the web interface, you do not need to calibrate or make any other changes.
